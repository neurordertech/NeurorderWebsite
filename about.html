About Neurorder

Building intelligent systems for a more inclusive economic future.

Neurorder is a South African research and technology company focused on Digital Public Infrastructure, artificial intelligence, alternative credit scoring, and community-centred innovation.

A new order for invisible economies.

The name Neurorder represents a new order shaped by intelligence, systems thinking, and practical technology. Our work focuses on people and communities who are often excluded from formal financial systems despite being active economic contributors.

We believe technology should not only serve large institutions. It should also help informal traders, small businesses, youth entrepreneurs, domestic workers, and underserved communities build visibility, trust, and opportunity.

What we focus on

Research

Exploring how AI, data, and digital infrastructure can support financial inclusion.

Systems

Designing practical, low-resource solutions that can work in real South African environments.

Community

Connecting technology with social development through the iNtsikelelo Foundation.
