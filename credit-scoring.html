WHAT THE SERVICE DOES

A clearer path from economic activity to financial understanding.

The service records information that you voluntarily provide, stores it securely within your authenticated account, and organises it into a financial history that may later support Neurorder research and scoring.

IMPORTANT SERVICE NOTICE

What Neurorder Financial Story is today.

This service is currently a research and infrastructure prototype. It allows authenticated users to create a financial profile and record voluntary monthly economic activity.

It does not currently issue loans, approve credit, replace a registered credit bureau or make a final bank lending decision.

Any score produced during the research stage should be treated as an experimental indicator only.
